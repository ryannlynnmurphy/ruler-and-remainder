The Reality Check

Paste an AI news story. It reads the gap between what the story claims and what is probably true — using the corpus's method (confidence–reality divergence, the three tiers, the remainder) and the twelve articles of the Cybersecurity Bill of Rights — and tells you the likely reality.

This one calls a model, so it needs a key. Bring your own. The call goes straight from your browser to Anthropic with your key; the key is stored only in your browser (if you tick remember). Nothing — not the story, not the key — touches this site. Data leaves consciously: you opt in, every time.
