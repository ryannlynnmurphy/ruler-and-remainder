The Reality Check

the anti-hype machine

Paste an AI news story. It reads the gap between what the story claims and what is probably true — using the corpus's method (confidence–reality divergence, the three tiers, the remainder) and the twelve articles of the Cybersecurity Bill of Rights — and tells you the likely reality.

This one calls a model, and the reading runs here, on the site — you don't bring anything. Be plain about what that means: your story is sent to Anthropic to be read, and a live web search is run to ground it against current sources — so the claim also reaches a search provider. Unless you say otherwise it joins the public ledger of checks at the foot of this page, on the record. Data leaves consciously — running a check is the opt-in, every time, and you can keep any single check off the ledger.
