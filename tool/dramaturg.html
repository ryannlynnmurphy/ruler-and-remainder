    This installs Ollama, starts it, and pulls the models Dorothy's local rung runs on. Everything stays on your machine. Paste it into a terminal:

    curl -fsSL …/setup-local.sh | sh
    ↓ download script installs qwen2.5 · llava

    Once it finishes, the dot turns green and the workstation routes there first. To use your node from the deployed site, expose it (Tailscale funnel) and set OLLAMA_URL.
