The CRD Audit

A runnable instrument. It treats a piece of marketing or technical copy as a model output and tests one condition: does the stated confidence exceed the architectural reality? It surfaces candidates — you complete the two-sentence test. It is calibrated to fire on the gap, not on the existence of something critical to say.

Runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you paste leaves the page — no server, no API, no log. Read the method →
