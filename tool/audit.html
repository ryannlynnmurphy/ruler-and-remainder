The CRD Audit

The method needs two sentences. A: the claim, from the copy. B: the architectural reality, from the spec or the code. It fires where A's confidence contradicts B — and it runs a second pass, checking the same copy against the twelve articles of the Cybersecurity Bill of Rights. Paste both; the audit does real work only once it has B.

Runs entirely in your browser. Nothing you paste leaves the page — no server, no API, no log. Read the method →
