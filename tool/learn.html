Learn the research

with the dramaturg

The dramaturg is a character whose whole craft is making abstract ideas legible — collapsing fog into the specific phrase, the actual sentence, the thing you can do. It teaches what this corpus is trying to teach. Bring a question, a half-formed idea, or nothing at all. There is no wrong place to start.

the dramaturg runs on a model, grounded in the corpus. it marks what's established, exploratory, or speculative — and it's still a model's read. you keep the pen.
